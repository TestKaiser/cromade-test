LIVING SPACE

Stunning Custom Living Space Designs and Layouts

Central living spaces are the nucleus of every home, with the potential to satisfy varying aspects of your lifestyle. Whether you are looking to build a new family room, library, home theatre, bedroom, or more, CroMade Cabinetry will design your living spaces to suit your every need.

From beautiful paneled libraries to practical entertainment units to elegant walk-in closets, CroMade’s custom cabinet designers will help you take your living space to where you’ve always dreamed it could be. It is our desire to help you achieve maximum comfort, style and functionality where you do most of your living.

TRADITIONAL

Traditional living spaces offer ornate detailing and visually appealing designs. Whether you’re looking for a new formal look in your library, walk-in closet, living room, or loft space, CroMade’s custom traditional living space cabinetry can make your classic dreams come true. We offer various hand-crafted traditional styles and colours in the finest hard materials.

 

 

TRANSITIONAL

Transitional living spaces offer a variety of traditional and modern elements. With simple storage solutions, ornate designs, and a manner of sleek sophistication, CroMade’s custom transitional living space cabinetry can take your most lived-in areas both back in time and ahead into the future. We offer minimalist functionality with elaborate patterns to create a space for you that transcends the test of time.

 

 

CONTEMPORARY

Contemporary living spaces offer functionality with sleek, clean lines. If you’re looking to update and de-clutter your living space, CroMade’s custom contemporary living space cabinetry can help you achieve your
less-is-more philosophy. We offer a variety of chic contemporary styles for your family room, bedroom, finished basement, and more.

 

 

MODERN

Similar to the contemporary living space, modern living spaces offer an air of clean sophistication. Whether you’re looking to streamline your living areas or amp up your storage space, CroMade’s custom modern living space cabinetry can help your living area feel like a home away from home. We offer functional-yet-stylish cabinetry for your home theatre, family room, walk-in closet, and more.
