KITCHENS

Beautiful Custom Kitchen Designs and Layout

Kitchen layouts are a veritable hybrid of design, blending both functionality and aesthetic appeal. CroMade Cabinetry aims to take you and your kitchen to an experience beyond typical kitchen designs, where your kitchen becomes a showcased work of art.

As homeowners who take pride in their surroundings, your vision of what a culinary space should encompass becomes our inspiration from which to turn your dreams into a practical and stunning kitchen space. It is our privilege to make your project a priority and to fulfill your dreams of building a remarkable custom kitchen.

TRADITIONAL

Traditional kitchen styles have a formal, elegant look, reminiscent of the 18th, 19th and 20th Century Western World. If you’re looking for culture in your cabinetry, CroMade’s custom traditional designs can help transform your kitchen space into a classic work of art. With ornate Victorian detailing, antique-like Edwardian fixtures, and elegant neoclassical motifs, CroMade’s traditional kitchen cabinets will carry old world charm into your 21st century home.

 

 

TRANSITIONAL

Transitional kitchen styles offer a balance between traditional and modern looks. With a blend of classic architectural elements and modern clean lines, transitional cabinetry boasts an air of timeless practicality. If you’re interested in updating your kitchen space with tailored lines and simplified elegance, CroMade’s custom transitional kitchen cabinet designs will help bring your kitchen into a realm of culinary comfort and aesthetic luxury.

 

 

CONTEMPORARY

Contemporary kitchen styles have a modern, minimalist feel with streamlined patterns and durable manufactured materials. If you’re looking for a clean, modern-day look in your cabinetry, CroMade’s custom contemporary kitchen cabinet designs can help convert your kitchen into a sleek, stylish, functional space where less is always more.

 

 

 

 

MODERN

Similar to contemporary designs, modern kitchen styles feature sleek lines with visually exciting materials and remarkable textures. Acrylics, metals, textured glass and exotic woods are just some of the elements that define the modern style. If you’re looking to build a kitchen that fits easily into your busy, modern lifestyle, CroMade’s custom modern kitchen cabinet designs will help usher your kitchen into a new era of warm sophistication.
