BATHROOMS

Outstanding Custom Bathroom Designs and Layouts

Bathrooms are multi-functional spaces, providing you with both practical and relaxing elements. CroMade Cabinetry designs aspire to create your perfect, affordable, spa-inspired hideaway where you can take a break from life’s biggest stresses in your own private sanctuary. Our commitment to you is to design a bathroom that will become your refuge from the demands of daily life.

Bathroom vanities, cabinets and custom designs are among our specialties. Your bathroom cabinets and vanities have endless possibilities with our host of beautiful styles and layouts. With CroMade’s custom cabinetry, you have a wide selection of wood cabinet finishes at your fingertips to transform your dream bathroom escape into a reality.

TRADITIONAL

Traditional bathrooms offer classic colonial designs made from solid, high-quality materials. If you’re searching for an elegant, heart-warming bathroom vanity, CroMade’s custom traditional bathroom cabinetry will help take your bathroom to the next level
of luxury.

 

 

TRANSITIONAL

Transitional bathrooms offer a mix of classic designs and contemporary appeal. Whether you’re looking for traditional elegance or modern functionality, CroMade’s custom transitional vanities will provide your bathroom with a look that evolves with your lifestyle.

 

 

CONTEMPORARY

Contemporary bathrooms offer sleek sophistication with minimalist designs. If you’re looking for a vanity that is both chic and functional, CroMade’s custom contemporary bathroom cabinetry can help style and simplify your every
bathroom need.

 

 

 

 

MODERN

Similar to contemporary styles, modern bathrooms offer clean lines, upscale materials and a spa-like feel. No matter what shape or size you bathroom happens to be, CroMade’s custom modern bathroom vanities will help create the simple, elegant bathroom retreat you’ve always dreamed of.
